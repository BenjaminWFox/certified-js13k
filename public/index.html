You are the GROUND worker! Work with your partner to fix the powerline. It should only take a minute, but small mistakes add up, and big ones send you to the hospital!

Your job is to:

  • Keep the birds off the line worker with your BB gun. (shoot w/ mouse)
  • Warn the line worker of incoming power surges. (watch for surges on the lower line!)

You need to watch out for:

  • The line worker warning you of oncoming trains. React too slow and its game-over!
  • Not shooting the line worker in the head. It's just BBs, but it hurts and adds to the timer!

You are the LINE worker! Work with your partner to fix the powerline. It should only take a minute, but small mistakes add up, and big ones send you to the hospital!

Your job is to:

  • Fix wires by typing the first letter of the color of the word shown. (use keybaord)
  • Send warning to the ground worker of oncoming trains. (watch for sound waves on the right!)

You need to watch out for:

  • The ground worker warning you of incoming surges. React too slow and its game-over!
  • Mis-identifying the colors! It adds to the timer!
